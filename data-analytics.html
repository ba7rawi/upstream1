Analytics & Reporting

Overview

At Upstream, we provide you with a single source of truth that is dependable, accurate and actionable. We present clear and reliable outputs that are user centric and that can enable staff across your organization to gain knowledge and unlock valuable insights to make informed and timely decisions.

What we deliver

Reporting-as-a-service: Only by giving your decision makers access to insights can they help you run a truly data-driven organization. This means scaling your analysis and reporting enterprise-wide, across disparate datasets, siloed functions and busy teams. Transforming your data into a tangible asset that works for you, underpinning your strategic and operational objectives.

Dynamic Dashboards: we supercharge our reports with dynamic dashboards prepared by our business analyst specialists who will meet with you and your team to understand your nees, recommend appropriate design and move on to execution with deadline respect and accountability as our values. Some of our used technology include : Power BI, Tableau, Streamlit and Dash.
